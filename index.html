ABOUT US

our vision

Our vision is our framework within which our employees work for rural development. Our vision statement guides us through every endeavor and helps achieve sustainable growth for every person involved with Vel electronics. We aim to become the top Green technology service provider offering a complete replacement for finite energy.

Our organization primarily focus on Research, Education, Sustainable Energy Product, Energy-efficient Products, Emergency Appliance, Industrial Systems, Mobile Devices, Travel and transportation and Wireless PAN providing a wide spectrum of design, development, testing and support services.

our Mission

  • To carry our company to global perspective with global competitive beyond the industry average for long period to serve better, and that company would grow in every year by giving solution to disappointment on need of electricity by perfectively utilize the renewable energy sources.
  • Our mission is to provide high quality Energy-efficient Embedded Products and Solutions that can largely reduce the cost and complexity of such requirements.
  • We believe in looking beyond technology and to reach out to industrialist at large through LED product, solar power system projects and to make a large improvement in energy efficiency.
  • To strive to be competitive institution through customer satisfaction and challenging institution by making opportunities in rural side.
  • Aim to improvement in the efficiency of services provided to customers through continual improvement of systems, processes and resources.

who we are

Vel electronics is an electronics product manufacturing industry that currently running in Venkateswarapuram, Tirunelveli district. It has highly educated professionals for management & research and well trained & experienced employees for manufacturing & service.

our profile

We strictly follow on dignity and quality in each and every aspect of our company. Our company slogan says two things, that’s

  • Developing the electricity from renewable & sustainable energy resources
  • Reducing the loss of power in electricity by advanced embedded controls
The major part of our product in particular, comparative performance is based on sustainable green energies and advanced technologies that reduce the loss of power. Currently we are manufacturing LED bulbs, Emergency lights, Embedded controls for street light and motor pump sets and Charger for rechargeable battery items. We also providing solar power systems for Home, Shop and industry. The management would try to carry out this company beyond the industry average with global competitive environment.
